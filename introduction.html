Welcome to IntelliTest!

IntelliTest is a Cognitive Analysis Platform designed to assess and enhance different aspects of cognitive functioning through a series of interactive tests. Each test evaluates a key area of mental agility, helping you gain deeper insights into your strengths and areas for improvement.

How It Works:

  • You will go through four unique tests, each targeting a specific cognitive ability.
  • Each test includes a timed assessment to measure your performance under pressure.
  • Before the main test, you can try a practice game to familiarize yourself with the mechanics.
  • Your scores will be calculated based on accuracy, speed, and efficiency.

Overview of Tests:

  • Memory Game: Tests your ability to recall and match patterns while minimizing moves.
  • Attention Test: Measures your focus and processing speed through the IDSST-S game.
  • Resilience Test: Evaluates your adaptability, problem-solving skills, and emotional regulation.
  • Deduction Quiz: Assesses logical reasoning and systematic analysis.

Why Take IntelliTest?

  • Identify cognitive strengths and weaknesses.
  • Improve decision-making, problem-solving, and focus.
  • Gain actionable insights to enhance mental resilience.
  • Track progress over time and optimize cognitive efficiency.
